ABOUT US



Hi, I'm Skyler! Originally from Illinois, I've made my way down to Houston, Texas, where I discovered a new passion that completely transformed my life—Pilates. From the very first class, I was hooked. Within weeks, I knew I wanted to become an instructor so I could share the same energy, joy, and empowerment that Pilates brought into my life.

As I dove deeper into the Pilates world, I noticed something missing—fun, expressive grip socks that matched the confidence and personality I was feeling in my classes. Most options were plain or uninspiring, and I wanted something that made me feel bold, stylish, and secure. So, I decided to design my own.

That idea turned into this shop. The Spoiled Bunny was born.

My mission is to help you feel confident and comfortable from the ground up. Whether you're gearing up for your first workout or simply want a little more grip in your everyday life, my socks bring personality, safety, and style all in one.

Thank you for being here. I'm so excited to share this journey and my designs with you.
