ABOUT US



Hello, my name is Skyler! I'm originally from Illinois but over the years, I found myself moving down to Houston, Texas. Last year, I started taking Pilates classes, and after my first class, I was hooked. I immediately added Pilates to my weekly workout schedule. One month into attending regular Pilates classes, I decided to become an instructor. I wanted to teach people why I love Pilates, how it has changed my life, and how it can change theirs. I finally found a fitness career to pursue along with my other passions. As I started my Pilates journey, I noticed that the grip socks on the market were boring and plain. I enjoy expressing myself through the clothes I wear, so it was most fitting to have socks that matched my energy. I started this shop because I wanted to share my fun designs with everyone. I wanted others to feel empowered for their first fitness class or feel secure on any slick floor. When I have a cute outfit on, it makes me feel good about walking anywhere. Something about unique grip socks gave me confidence. Knowing I looked good made me feel good too. So, I designed and created my line of grip socks. Nowhere else can you find grippies like the ones in my shop. My socks are meant for fitness classes, adding safety, and for your everyday life outfits. My priority is to provide excellent customer service and high-quality products. Every product is drawn out and designed by me, created with care and attention to detail. Whether it's an item for yourself or a gift for a loved one, I put my heart and soul into every one of my products. What you can expect from my shop is that feeling of uniqueness and confidence you will receive when you put on your first pair of grip socks from my shop. My socks provide protection, safety, and style all in one. My experience - 700 hours of active teaching classes, 120 days of teaching pilates classes. My socks are unique and stand out in a good way.
